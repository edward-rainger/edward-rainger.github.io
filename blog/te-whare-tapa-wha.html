Te Whare Tapa Whā

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

Goals:

  • Improve gut health and strength.
  • Maintain a healthy diet.
  • Engage in regular physical activity to strengthen muscles and improve cardiovascular health.
  • Sleep well.

Plan:

  • Physical Activity: Walk everyday, lift weights 3x a week.
  • Nutrition: Plan meals in advance and resist urges to binge on rubbish.
  • Sleep: Aim for 8 hours of sleep each night. Wake up at same time each morning.

Taha Hinengaro (Mental Wellbeing)

Goals:

  • Create emotional intelligence.
  • Manage stress.
  • Build self-awareness and resilience.
  • Maximise dopamine (healthily).

Plan:

  • Journalling, time to myself and mindfulness daily.
  • Sharing problems, or just sharing in general (problem shared is a problem halved), counselling etc
  • Engage in healthy activites that I enjoy, complete small task throughout day.

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

Goals:

  • Seek purpose and identity
  • Feel fulfilled and present
  • Be a good person.

Plan:

  • Find gratitude: Take the time out of my day to notice the small things, and the things I take for granted.
  • Nature connection: Spend more time in environments that make me feel good e.g grounding at the beach.

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)

Goals:

  • Strengthen relationships with my family and friends
  • Look to be someone who helps others.
  • Be a calm presence

Plan:

  • Work on building trust that may have been wittled away in the past.
  • Volunteering, or doing things that help people without expecting anything in return.
  • Use the mindfulness practise to become a calm, stable presence.
